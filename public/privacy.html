Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 13, 2026

Welcome to Uncle Healthy. Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy specifically outlines what data we collect, how we collect it, and all the ways we use it when you use the Uncle Healthy iOS and web application.

AI Functionality & Data Sharing: We do NOT share your personal data with any third-party AI services. The in-app AI functionality acts solely as a general FAQ tool and does not collect, process, or transmit user data (including health, fitness, or contact information) to external AI servers.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information, which may be linked to your identity, to provide and improve our services:

Data Category Specific Data Collected How It Is Used
Contact Info Name, Phone Number App Functionality, Analytics, Product Personalization
Health & Fitness Health Data, Fitness Data App Functionality, Product Personalization
User Content Customer Support Inquiries, Other User Content App Functionality, Product Personalization
Identifiers User ID, Device ID App Functionality, Product Personalization

Usage Data Product Interaction Analytics
Diagnostics Crash Data, Performance Data Analytics, App Functionality

2. How We Collect Data

We collect data through the following methods:

  • Direct Input: Data you manually provide when creating an account, filling out your profile, interacting with customer support, or logging health and fitness metrics.
  • Automated Collection: Data collected automatically when you use our app, such as device identifiers, app crash logs, performance metrics, and product interaction telemetry.
  • System APIs: Data accessed via Apple HealthKit or other device integrations (only when explicit permissions are granted by you on your device).

3. How We Use Data

The data we collect is utilized strictly for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To allow you to log in securely, track your fitness journey, process in-app purchases, and access customer support.
  • Product Personalization: To tailor the Uncle Healthy experience, app content, and suggested health metrics to your specific profile.
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our product so we can continuously improve the app's stability, design, and features based on crash and performance data.

4. Data Sharing and Protection

Uncle Healthy does not sell your personal information. We only share information with critical infrastructure and service providers required to run our app (such as Firebase for secure database, authentication, and hosting). Any third-party infrastructure providers we use provide the same or equal protection of user data as outlined in this policy. As explicitly stated above, we never share personal profile, health, or contact data with third-party AI platforms.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time through the in-app settings menu or by contacting our support team.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us through the in-app support channels.
